Welcome to Fake News at City of Westminster College!

Fake news is false or misleading information presented as news. It often has the aim of damaging the reputation of a person or entity, or making money through advertising revenue. In the colleges case, fake news can damage the schools reputation when it comes to key factors. Some examples are the false accusation that the saftey of the students is not ensured, or that students here at the college do not revieve a high enough pass rate. Fake news like this can reaaly put a dent in the colleges reputation and it needs to be stopped!
